Overcoming adversity

Encouraging new perspectives is what Jennie does best. Looking at personal and professional development in a unique way. Jennie is able to tailor her speech depending on who she is talking to, meaning that she makes the most effective impact on a wide range of audiences. Adversity comes in all shapes and sizes whether we embrace it or not it is part of life. Learning to rise to the challenge is what builds character and resilience.

How does she muster the courage to persevere in the face of sheer adversity? Do we all have the capacity to overcome adversity? How does she stay strong? How does she keep going? You will be uplifted by the remarkable story of Jennie. Imagine being able to apply some of her philosophies of courage, dedication, and strength to your own life.
